J Trix, also known as Jaitley Bhardwaj, a rapper and songwriter from Kolkata, recently premiered the third single from his upcoming "Middle Class Boys" EP with Def Jam Recordings India. Produced by Subspace, the single titled "I Wish" follows his previous release "Sexy" and a collaboration with Ikka on "Gundagardi." Alongside the audio release, Trix unveiled a visually stunning music video directed by Upasya Mukhopadhyay, showcasing his dominance in West Bengal's rap scene. Known for his hard-hitting rap style, J Trix has been consistently dropping tracks this year, including the Subspace-produced "Legacy," where he takes aim at "autotune rappers," and "Banger Pro Max." With over 40 singles and an EP to his name, J Trix has become a prominent figure in the Indian rap scene, particularly in Bengal. Def Jam Recordings India's collaboration with J Trix on "I Wish" highlights their efforts to promote regional rap music in India. The label has also partnered with other regional artists like Marathi emcee Sambata for "Sod Lacharicha Paksh" and West Delhi's Chaar Diwaari for "Violence" featuring Bombay Lokal's rapper Gravity. In August 2023, Def Jam Recordings India organized the "50 Years of Hip-Hop" concert across Indian cities, leaving a significant impact on the country's rap scene. J Trix's involvement in this event further solidifies his position as a key player in Indian hip-hop. Overall, J Trix's latest single and collaboration with Def Jam Recordings India mark another milestone in his career, showcasing his talent and contribution to the flourishing rap culture in India.
