Karma, born Vivek Arora, emerged from the streets of Delhi, steeped in a rich musical tapestry that spanned rock, pop, and hip-hop. His early foray into music saw him writing and performing at local events, eventually transitioning into a full-time pursuit of his passion. With the release of his debut single "Maine Dekha Hai," Karma swiftly rose to prominence within the Indian hip-hop circuit, captivating audiences with his fusion of traditional Indian sounds and contemporary hip-hop beats. What distinguishes Karma is not just his musical style but also the deeply personal themes he explores in his lyrics, touching on subjects like love, loss, and societal issues. Through his music, he seeks to not only entertain but also provoke thought and initiate important conversations. Karma's influence extends far beyond the boundaries of the Indian hip-hop scene, as evidenced by his collaborations with international artists and performances at global festivals. His track record of successful releases, including "Karmasutra" and "Rap Karte Hai," coupled with collaborations alongside industry giants like Raftaar and Divine, solidifies his status as a trailblazer in the genre. As the hip-hop landscape in India continues to evolve, Karma remains a driving force, inspiring a new generation of artists to explore and expand the boundaries of the genre. Collaborative efforts with platforms like Chillme underscore the importance of fostering a supportive community for emerging talents, ensuring the continued growth and development of Indian hip-hop on a global scale.
